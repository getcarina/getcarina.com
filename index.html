Features

With Carina by Rackspace, users get a “zero infrastructure” hosted container environment. You save time by no longer worrying about building, managing, and updating your container environment.

Performance

Built for speed - with no hypervisor overhead or additional complexity, you avoid the “hypervisor tax” on performance and are able to deploy & run applications as much as 20% faster.

Container-Native Tools

Instant access to the Docker toolchain you already know and work with. With this, you gain portability across providers, form-factors and more reducing errors and saving time

Easy to Use

Provides easy and fast installation, choice of user interfaces to create clusters, best-practice documentation, guides and architectures, as well as community support direct from other developers.

At Your Fingertips

With Carina, we're proud that we've streamlined the entire experience to allow you to get started with Docker & Docker Swarm in as little as 45 seconds.

Everything you need to get started

Carina makes containers more accessible to everyone, from novices to professional developers, with best-practice guides, documentation, and the ability to get started in minutes. Check out our wide randge of tutorials, best practices and tips & tricks to go from "zero to hero" in no time.
